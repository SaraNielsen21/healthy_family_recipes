Appetizers

Here are a few low-fat appetizers for you to try out!

  • Baked Mini Corndogs
    • This United States dish is a delicacy among a majority of the States. Coated and grilled over an open flame. Doesn't get much better!
  • Pico De Gallo
    • Made with home-grown tomatoes, onions, and mild peppers. Sprinkled with cilantro, you're taste buds will be thanking you!
  • Pineapple Salsa
    • Made with foreign-grown tomatoes, onions, spicy peppers, and pineapple. This spicy-sweet concoction will leave your taste buds screaming for more!
  • Veggie Pizza
    • A twist on the old pizza pie. Mushrooms, green peppers, olives, tomatoes, and onions come together under a roof of mozzarella cheese.
  • Mediterranean Dip
    • Love dip? Come no further! Cucumber, tomato, and hummus mix together and are topped with feta cheese and parsley for this one of a kind Mediterranean dip!
